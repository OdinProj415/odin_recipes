Kapsilon Shawarma

Makes 2 servings
Total time : 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 175g plant based shawarma (e.g. vivera plant shawarma)
  • 350g oven chips
  • ½ a red onion
  • ⅓ of a cucumber
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 50 g plant-based grated cheese
  • 100g sliced iceberg lettuce
  • plant-based garlic sauce

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 ËšC. Spread the chips over a baking tray covered with baking paper, and sprinkle with salt if desired. Bake for about 25 minutes, until golden brown.
  2. Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan, and fry the shawarma for 6 minutes, until golden brown.
  3. Spread the shawarma over the fries. Sprinkle with the plant-based cheese, and place in the oven for about 10 minutes, until the cheese has melted.
  4. Cut the onion into thin rings, the tomatoes into 1-cm cubes, and the cucumbers into half-slices.
  5. Remove the dish from the oven and cover with the lettuce, cucumber, diced tomatoes, and red onion. Drizzle with the plant-based garlic sauce.
